TrueProfit 💹

Know your real profit.  A lightweight, single-file side hustle calculator built for Nigerian business owners and entrepreneurs worldwide. 

Most business owners only count what they see. TrueProfit counts everything, including the hidden costs (data, delivery, platform fees) you might be forgetting to get your true net margin.

Features
Smart Calculation: Deducts COGS, labor, and hidden expenses to reveal true profit, net margin, and ROI.

Profit Insights: Auto-generates smart, actionable business advice based on your margins.

Scenario Testing: Compare "Scenario A" vs "Scenario B" side-by-side to see how price hikes or cost cuts impact your bottom line.

History & Trends: Save reports locally and visualize your profit growth over time with a built-in trend chart.

Local Storage: 100% private. All your financial data is saved directly to your browser's local storage.

Tech Stack
HTML5
CSS3 (Custom variables, responsive grid/flexbox)
Vanilla JavaScript (DOM manipulation, Charting via Canvas API, LocalStorage)
Zero external dependencies (Single-file architecture)

How to Run
Since this is a vanilla HTML/JS app contained entirely in `index.html`, you don't need a build step or package manager. 
1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. Alternatively: View the live deployment via Vercel/GitHub Pages.
